13.2 Types of Rooms

The double room is sometimes composed of two single beds and sometimes a large bed. The so-called "single" room is an individual room occupied by one person. The triple room is sometimes composed of two single beds and an "extra bed," and the quadruple room of two single beds and two "extra beds." FlyOmra (Dogan Voyages) cannot guarantee the location of the room in the hotel.

13.3 Meals

All services (breakfast, lunch, dinner) that are not specified in the description are not included in the reserved packages.

13.4 Transfer

The type of transfer included in the price is mentioned in the travel booklet. There are two types of transfers: collective and private. Drivers do not speak French.
